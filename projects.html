Office Green: Plant Pals

Goal: Develop and manage a project to increase brand awareness for a plant subscription service.

Project Overview

This project focuses on clarifying business goals, defining scope, and creating a structured plan to increase awareness of Office Green. As a project manager, my role is to connect stakeholders, define measurable objectives, and keep the project on track.

Key Responsibilities

  • Define S.M.A.R.T. goals for brand awareness.
  • Create a project charter and scope statement.
  • Identify and analyze key stakeholders.
  • Develop a high-level timeline and milestones.

Tools & Methods

  • Project charter and RACI chart
  • Risk register and issue log
  • Communication plan for stakeholders
  • Milestone-based Gantt-style schedule

Community Center Conversion

Goal: Convert a retired middle school into a fully operational community center and library.

Project Overview

This project involves planning the transformation of an existing building into a community hub. It includes setting up a community library, garden, staffing structure, and feedback mechanisms to measure impact on residents.

Key Responsibilities

  • Define project scope, including in-scope and out-of-scope elements.
  • Plan the library launch, community garden, and staffing structure.
  • Develop a timeline to reach full operation within 12–15 months.
  • Design resident surveys to assess community impact.

Tools & Methods

  • Work breakdown structure (WBS)
  • Timeline and dependency mapping
  • Stakeholder analysis (community members, city, partners)
  • Feedback loop via resident surveys
